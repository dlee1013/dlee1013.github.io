About me

I'm a 23 year old college graduate and was born in Dubai. I moved to Chandler Arizona at six years old, a city southeast of Phoenix and have lived here ever since.

I've always been exposed to technology at a young age. I'm an avid gamer and have been gaming since I was in elementary school with handheld devices like Nintendo Gameboys. As I got older, I started getting into PC gaming and I became more interested in how computers operated and along with my dad being a software engineer, my curiosity for computers and programming grew which then led me to majoring in software engineering. Eventually, I graduated from Arizona State University with a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering.

During my senior year in college, we were given the opportunity to work with a company as software developers and aid them in any software projects they needed to accomplish to help advance their product they were developing. I got the chance to work with a small startup company, Fenoto Technologies and we created an Android application and a Google Chrome extension to display images from your local device or using the web to one of their e-paper tablet devices. Their overarching goal was to develop a solution for the use and consumption of paper. Paper advertisements you see in public in the mall and bus stops as well as calendars used for store schedules or paper calendars you hang up on your fridge were the use cases specifically targeted in replacing with an e-paper tablet. I was exposed to wireframing using Adobe XD, using Android Studio, and working with JavaScript to code a google chrome extension.

Outside of programming, I enjoy playing basketball. I've been playing basketball since I was in 1st grade and played all the way into high school where I played on the varsity team. Now I play video games during most of my free time. I also like to listen to a lot of music and watch the NBA. Go Suns!
