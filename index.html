About me

I'm a 26-year-old college graduate who was born in Dubai. I moved to Chandler, Arizona—a city southeast of Phoenix—at six years old and have lived here ever since.

I've always been exposed to technology from a young age. I'm an avid gamer and have been playing video games since elementary school. As I got older and technology advanced, I became especially fascinated with PC gaming. That naturally sparked a deeper curiosity about computers and, more specifically, software programming. With my father working in the field as well, it felt like a natural path to follow. In May of 2021, I graduated from Arizona State University with a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering.

During my senior year of college, I had the opportunity to work with a startup company, Fenoto Technologies, as part of a capstone project. We created an Android application and a Google Chrome extension that allowed users to display images from any internet-connected device to one of their e-paper tablet displays. Their goal was to provide a paperless solution for things like advertisements at malls or bus stops, in-store calendars, or even the paper calendars people hang on their fridge. I gained experience wireframing with Adobe XD, developing Android apps with Android Studio, and building a Chrome extension using JavaScript.

After graduating, I joined FedEx as a Software Developer, where I’ve been working since May 2022. My role involves building and maintaining scalable Java-based microservices using Spring Boot, developing RESTful APIs that support logistics and tracking systems, and implementing CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins and GitHub Actions. I've also worked with Redis Streams for distributed data communication and contributed across the full software development lifecycle in an Agile Scrum environment. The experience has deepened my understanding of enterprise-level architecture and team collaboration in a fast-paced setting.

Outside of programming, I enjoy playing basketball. I’ve been playing since 1st grade and continued through high school, where I played on the varsity team. These days, I spend most of my free time gaming, hanging out with friends, listening to music, and watching the NBA. Go Suns!
